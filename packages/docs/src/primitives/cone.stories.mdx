# cone

The `cone` element creates a truncated cone. It has a `height` in the z-axis, a radius at `z = 0` of `radius1` and a radius at `z = height` of `radius2`. An optional `angle` prop limits the shape to the portion of the cone within that angle around the z-axis. The cone can optionally be centered on the origin.
