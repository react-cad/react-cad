# union

The `union` element creates a shape that is the sum of its children. It takes no props.

<br/><br/>

## Examples

<br />

### Union of a cube and a sphere

<Source language="jsx" code={`
<union>
  <box x={2} y={2} z={2} center />
  <sphere radius={Math.sqrt(2)} />
</union>
`} />
