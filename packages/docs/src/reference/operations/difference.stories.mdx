# difference

The `difference` element takes its first child and subtracts all the the other children from it. It takes no props.

<br/><br/>

## Examples

<br />

### Difference of a cube and a sphere

A sphere subtracted from a cube.

<Source language="jsx" code={`
<difference>
  <box x={2} y={2} z={2} center />
  <sphere radius={Math.sqrt(2)} />
</difference>
`} />

<Story name="difference" args={{ children: [
  <box x={2} y={2} z={2} center />,
  <sphere radius={Math.sqrt(2)} />
]}}>
  {Difference.bind({})}
</Story>

### Difference of a sphere and a cube

The same shapes as above, but with the cube subtracted from the sphere.

<Source language="jsx" code={`
<difference>
  <sphere radius={Math.sqrt(2)} />
  <box x={2} y={2} z={2} center />
</difference>
`} />

<Story name="difference opposite" args={{ children: [
  <sphere radius={Math.sqrt(2)} />,
  <box x={2} y={2} z={2} center />
]}}>
  {Difference.bind({})}
</Story>
